5. Consider the data below on the effects of a particular treatment for solid tumors patients from a double-blind clinical trial. The response is IMPROVEMENT measured by RECIST (Response Evaluation Criteria in Solid Tumors). RECIST is a set of published rules that define when cancer patients improve (“respond”), stay the same (“stabilize”), or worsen (“progression”) during treatments.

  1. Conduct a hypothesis, at 5% level of significance, to determine whether GENDER adding significantly to the predictive ability of the model. State hypothesis, test statistics and draw conclusion.
    Solution
    \(H_0: \beta_{gender} = 0\)
    \(H_a: \beta_{gender} \neq 0\)
    T.S.: Wald Test \[ \begin{align*} \frac{\beta_{gender} - 0}{SE(\hat{\beta}_{gender})} &\sim N(0, 1) \\ &= \frac{1.2820}{0.5693} \\ &= 0.0243 \end{align*} \] p-value = 0.0243.
